# Data & Privacy

We take the security and privacy of your data seriously.

## Tenant isolation

Each organization operates in its own isolated **tenant**. Your data is not
shared with or visible to other organizations on the platform.

## Compliance

The platform is built and operated to SOC 2 controls covering security,
availability, and confidentiality. We anticipate achieving **SOC 2 Type 1**
certification by the end of Q2 and **SOC 2 Type 2** by the end of Q3.

Beyond SOC 2, we intend to pursue additional certifications to build trust —
including HIPAA and ISO 42001, among others. If your organization has a specific
compliance requirement, let us know through
[Naviant Customer Support](https://naviant.com/customer-support/).

## Data handling

- Data is encrypted in transit.
- Access to your data is restricted to authorized users in your organization.
- Operational access by our team follows least-privilege principles and is
  logged.

For detailed contractual terms regarding data processing, refer to your
